Description of Mission:
Lupis Two takes you into the somewhat newly
infested Lupis system. This level excellent
for individual or deathmatch play. Remember,
some times it is better to grab the loot and
run, so that you can live to fight again some
day. Lupis Two is a challenge to the beginner
and the experienced alike. Large design and
fully multiplayer supported.
