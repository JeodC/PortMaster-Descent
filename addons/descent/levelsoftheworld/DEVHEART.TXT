Title: Devil's Heart
Author: Nathan Aultman & Brown S. R. Smith
EMail: Crimsonarc@AOL.com

Description:

Designed to be wicked for single or multi-player,
this level has some interesting tricks and puzzles. 
Designed to test your staying power.  Best played
one player, or as a Multi-player anarchy.  Co-op play
is fairly easy unless you are playing on Ace or above.
