Description: Here's a chance to chase

Author: Tim Fredericy
Date: Aug. 28, 1995

Description of Mission: Ever wonder if you
could outrun a homing missile? Well here's
your chance. There's plenty of room, hiding
places, weapons, and ammo to make this level
last forever, if your opponents let you live that
long.  This level was created with multi-
player in mind.
