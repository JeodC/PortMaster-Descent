Computer Gaming World is the oldest and largest computer game magazine in the 
world. Founded in 1981 by Russell Sipe, the magazine has consistently been 
the leading authority in computer games for almost 15 years. Now published by 
Ziff-Davis Publishing Company, Computer Gaming Worldappears on newsstands as 
a print edition, on AT&T Interchange as a full electronic edition, on the 
World Wide Web as a partial edition, and on CD-ROM as a supplement to the 
print magazine.
