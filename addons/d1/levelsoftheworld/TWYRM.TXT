Name of Mission:  The Tapeworm Colony

Description of mission:
The Naustic Tapeworm Colony has fallen
victim to the revolting robots rampage. 
A few of the remaining colonists are
transmitting distress signals via an
obscure frequency. It is up to you and your
Pyro GX to rescue the remaining colonists
and then make your way through winding
confusion of the Tapeworm Colony.
